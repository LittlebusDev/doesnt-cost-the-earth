ABOUT US

Founded in the picturesque landscapes of New Zealand, "Doesn't Cost the Earth" was born out of a deep love for the environment and a passion for fashion. We recognized the need for a brand that seamlessly blends eco-friendliness, affordability, and trendsetting designs. With this vision in mind, we set out to create a brand that empowers you to make responsible choices without compromising on your personal style.

ECO-FRIENDLY

We take pride in our commitment to the planet. Our journey towards sustainability involves careful selection of materials that are not only kind to your skin but also gentle on the Earth. From organic cotton to recycled fabrics, each piece of clothing is thoughtfully crafted with the environment in mind.

FASHIONABLE

Fashion is an expression of individuality, and we want you to feel your best while making a positive impact. Our team of talented designers stays ahead of the latest trends to offer you a curated collection that speaks to your unique style. And the best part? It's affordable, ensuring that sustainability remains within everyone's reach.

JOIN OUR MISSION

When you choose "Doesn't Cost the Earth," you're joining a movement of like-minded individuals who believe in making responsible choices. Together, we can transform the world of fashion into one that celebrates both personal style and environmental consciousness. Thank you for being a part of our journey. Together, we're proving that being eco-friendly "Doesn't Cost the Earth."
